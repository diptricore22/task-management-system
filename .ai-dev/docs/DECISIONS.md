# 📓 Architecture Decision Records (ADR)

> **A log of significant architectural and technology decisions made during the project.  
> Every important "why" should be recorded here so future developers (or AI) understand the reasoning.**

---

## ADR Format

Each ADR captures:
- **Context**: What situation required a decision?
- **Decision**: What was decided?
- **Rationale**: Why was this decided over alternatives?
- **Consequences**: What are the trade-offs?
- **Status**: `Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-XXX`
